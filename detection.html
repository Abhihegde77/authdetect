About Authorities Detection

Authorities detection in X-ray images is a critical process in maintaining security across various sectors. Our advanced AI-powered system can quickly and accurately identify potential threats or anomalies in X-ray scans, helping security personnel make informed decisions and ensure safety.

The AuthDetect system uses state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms trained on vast datasets of X-ray images. It can detect a wide range of authorities, including weapons, explosives, drugs, and other contraband items, with high precision and minimal false positives.
