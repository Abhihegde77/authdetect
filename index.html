What are Authorities?

Authorities in X-ray images refer to foreign objects or anomalies that shouldn't be present in a normal scan. These can include hidden weapons, contraband, or other potentially dangerous items. Detecting authorities is crucial for maintaining security in various settings, such as airports, border crossings, and high-security facilities.
